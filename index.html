About

I have many interests and ideas in life - thus why I call myself an entrepreneur. My current ventures include offering graphic design and website development services through my business, 2 Fish Creative, LLC, and operating an Etsy shop offering printed goods for your special life events. The fun doesn't stop there though. I'm always thinking about the next big idea, while keeping an open mind if the right job opportunity comes my way.

While it sometimes seems like it, my life isn't all work and no play. When I'm not working on my latest project or learning a new technology, I look forward to the days I get outside to go fishing and soak up some vitamin D, breaks from behind the computer to tend to the chickens and garden, or getting out to play some hockey. I also love to cook. I spend a good amount of time in the kitchen home-cooking every meal. I often wonder how I find the time to do it all!
